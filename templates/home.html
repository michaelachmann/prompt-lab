Introduction

PrompLab is a web application designed for researchers and developers working with LLMs and ML models. Our goal is to simplify the documentation and evaluation process, providing a seamless experience for managing prompts, models, datasets, and experiments.

How It Works

  1. Create an account and log in to PrompLab.
  2. Start by adding your LLM prompts, models, and datasets.
  3. Conduct your experiments and document the results within the platform.
  4. Analyze and review your experiments through our intuitive interface.
